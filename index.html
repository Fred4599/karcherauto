Frequently Asked

Answers to the most common questions from Nampa drivers.

How long does a typical collision repair take?
Repair time varies by the extent of the damage. Minor repairs can often be completed in 1–3 days, while major collision work may take 1–2 weeks. We'll give you an accurate timeline upfront so you can plan ahead.
Do you work with insurance companies?
Yes. We work with most major insurance providers and can help navigate the claims process. Our goal is to make repairs as smooth and stress-free as possible for you.
Can you match my car's original paint color?
Absolutely. We use the latest color-mixing and matching technology to achieve a factory-perfect finish. Whether your car is a classic or a brand-new model, the paint will blend seamlessly.
Do you offer free estimates?
Yes — estimates are always free at Karcher Auto Body. Give us a call at (208) 463-9090 or stop by 2302 W. Karcher Rd. in Nampa, Monday through Friday, 7:30am–5:00pm.
